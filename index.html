About Us

We are two mechanical engineering students studying at the University of Melbourne. We started 3D printing as a hobby, and quickly found problems within the industry. For many, this technology was prohibitively expensive, and a steep learning curve was required to operate the machines.

We are here to provide an affordable, streamlined 3D printing service to the general public. We take orders from anyone, whether you are a university student needing models printed, or someone who likes to design models but does not have access to a 3D printer.

The maximum size we are able to print is 33 x 37 x 50 cm.

We offer a wide range of services including:

  • PLA Printing
  • PETG Printing
  • ABS Printing
  • Basic 3D Modelling

Email us your .stl file to mk3dprinting@gmail.com and we will be able to give you a free quote. We have a minimum order of $15.

Services

Available in a wide range of materials.

PLA Printing

The default go-to material for the majority of 3D printing. Great for producing high quality architectural models or engineering prototypes with fast turnaround.

Available colours: White, Black, Grey, Red, Orange, Blue, Pink, Gold

PETG Printing

Tougher prints compared to PLA, with higher heat resistance. Ideal for structural applications or where print will be subject to higher temperatures. Printing times typically 50% more compared to PLA print times.

ABS Printing

ABS finds its use in special cases where heat resistance is crucial. ABS is able to withstand up to 100 degrees celcius without deformation, and is typically only used in medium stress, high temp applications. Mostly superceded by PETG, due to its limitations in the shapes it is able to be printed.

Basic 3D Modelling

We are able to model up any ideas you have and turn them into reality. Accurate drawings of the object you wish to model are appreciated, although not required. Service is not suitable for artistic/natural modelling.

Colour

We offer a wide range of colours for PLA. If you require a different colour, we can order it in for you at no extra cost. There will, however, be a 3-4 day increase in processing time.

Announcement

Current processing time is 2-3 days. During busy assessment periods processing time may increase to 4-5 days.

Pickup location is either at the University of Melbourne or Bundoora.
